Project Vision

PolyGuard is an advanced IoT-based Smart Greenhouse management system designed to empower farmers with real-time telemetry and 24/7 autonomous micro-climate control. Developed as a final year engineering milestone, it integrates Firebase Real-time Database, ESP32 processing, and a professional-grade React dashboard to bridge the gap between traditional farming and precision agriculture.
