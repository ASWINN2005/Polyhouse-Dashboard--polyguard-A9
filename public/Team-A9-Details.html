Project Vision

The Automated Smart Polyhouse Control System is an advanced IoT-based management platform designed to empower farmers with real-time telemetry and 24/7 autonomous micro-climate control. Developed as a final year engineering milestone within the Department of ECE, Kuppam Engineering College (Autonomous), Andhra Pradesh.
